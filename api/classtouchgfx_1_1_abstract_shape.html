Detailed Description

Simple widget capable of drawing a abstractShape.

The abstractShape can be scaled and rotated around 0,0. The shapes points (corners) are calculated with regards to scaling and rotation to allow for faster redrawing. Care must be taken to call updateAbstractShapeCache() after updating the shape, the scale of the shape or the rotation of the shape.
